What you'll learn
Explain the purpose and goals of harassment prevention training and the role of individuals and organizations in creating a safe and inclusive work environment
Define sexual harassment and its various forms, identify risk factors, and describe the potential impacts on individuals and the workplace
Differentiate between verbal, visual, and physical harassment and recognize associated behaviors
Identify and classify types of harassment, including hostile work environment harassment, quid pro quo harassment, and third-party harassment
Describe reporting procedures and legal protections for individuals reporting harassment
Description
This course covers all harassment prevention training mandates for non-supervisors in all U.S. states. Workplace harassment and discrimination are serious issues that can affect employees' well-being and productivity. Training helps ensure that all employees understand their role in maintaining a respectful and inclusive work environment. This course will guide employees through strategies, solutions, and best practices for identifying, reporting, and preventing harassment in their workplace.
